About Me

Short Biography

I am a software professional with nearly two decades of experience working as a developer and architect in Australia, Singapore and India. I have worked with all the major web and mobile platforms since the first days of Ruby on Rails, ASP.NET MVC, iOS, Android, Windows Phone and Windows 8. I appreciate beauty of design and user experience research that makes an app or a website stand out from others. I often dream of transitioning to a devigner (Developer + Designer) from what I am.

In 2007, I co-founded Liana Solutions, a boutique software design and development studio based in Sydney, Australia. My role in Liana Solutions is to provide leadership in all things technical. Liana Solutions provides consultancy services and product development resources in mobile, web and telephony applications.

Being a licensed Amateur Radio operator, I get on the air whenever propagation is good. Amateur Radio encourages me to dabble in electronics, including Arduinos, Beaglebones and Raspberry Pis.
